Call for Scientific Session Proposals

Deadline: Thursday, May 11, 2017 11:59 p.m. PT

Advancing Medicine: Bench to Bedside

In response to the rapid developments in the field of translational medicine, AAAS will be holding its first Science Translational Medicine conference on February 12-15, 2018 in Austin, Texas.

Since the founding of Science Translational Medicine in 2009, research dedicated to bridging the gap between basic research and human health application has accelerated. The speculations and first glimpses at potentially groundbreaking therapies that were featured in our first issue are now becoming reality. In a time when government funding for medical research is uncertain, we feel a dire need to facilitate discussion around the importance of translational biomedical research in advancing healthcare to the next level. We invite members of the biomedical research community to submit proposals for sessions to be featured in our upcoming conference. The deadline for all submissions is Thursday, May 11, 2017 at 11:59 p.m. PT. Decisions will be announced in early August 2017 after review and final selection by the Scientific Advisory Board of Science Translational Magazine.

About Science Translational Medicine

Science Translational Medicine (STM) is a growing interdisciplinary journal published by the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS). STM aims to encourage the collaborative, forward-looking mindset that will allow for more efficient applications of basic science to the understanding and treatment of human disease. The journal, available in print and online, features peer-reviewed research articles and commentary from highly regarded experts across all relevant areas of biomedical research.

Instructions for Submitting Scientific Session Proposals

All proposals MUST be submitted online before the deadline through the AAAS proposal submission website at www.aaas.org/meetings

The deadline for proposal submission is Thursday, April 20, 2017 at 11:59 p.m. PT. Proposals will not be accepted after the deadline. All proposals will be peer-reviewed. Decisions will be announced in July. To receive notifications, please ensure that the following email addresses will not be blocked: meetings@aaas.org, tlohwate@aaas.org, cjones@aaas.org, nmaylett@aaas.org, ekimbrel@aaas.org.

Follow these instructions carefully. The information you provide will be used by reviewers to evaluate and score your proposal. Incomplete proposals will be eliminated from consideration.

The AAAS Scientific Program Committee underscores that a successful proposal is characterized by interesting and timely topics that are thoughtfully developed and include capable and articulate speakers who represent the diversity of science and society, including disciplinary field, gender, ethnicity, geographic location, and institution.

SCHEDULING NOTE: When selecting speakers, please ensure that they are available to participate at any time from Friday, February 16 through the morning of Monday, February 19, 2018. Once the schedule for sessions is announced in the fall of 2017, it will be considered final.

LANGUAGE AND STYLE: Meeting attendees come from more than 50 countries. For language choice, use American English spelling and translations. For style, use The Chicago Manual of Style. Spell out all abbreviations and acronyms. Do not use "ALL CAPS" for the title of a symposium or the title of a speaker presentation.
